\begin{theorem}
Use the Stirling formula to show that
\begin{equation*}
\frac{a^n}{n!}\sim\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi n}}
\left(\frac{ae}{n}\right)^n~~~(n\to\infty)
\end{equation*}
when a is any constant, and deduce that
\[ \lim_{n \to \infty}\frac{a^n}{n!}=0\]
\end{theorem}
